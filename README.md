# Housing Market Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

The **Housing Market Analysis Dashboard** is a Power BI project developed to analyze historical housing market trends, property pricing, and regional sales performance. The dashboard transforms raw housing transaction data into interactive visualizations that help identify market patterns, compare property values, and evaluate real estate performance across different regions.

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can support data-driven decision-making in the real estate industry by providing insights into pricing trends, property characteristics, and market dynamics.

---

## Business Problem

Real estate organizations, investors, and analysts require accurate market insights to understand pricing behavior and sales trends.

This project was developed to answer business questions such as:

- Which regions have the highest property prices?
- How have housing prices changed over time?
- Which property types generate higher sales values?
- What is the difference between offer price and purchase price?
- How do economic indicators influence housing prices?

## Key Insights

- Property prices vary significantly across different regions.
- Purchase prices generally remain below initial offer prices.
- Larger properties tend to have higher market values.
- Price per square meter differs considerably based on property type and location.
- Economic indicators such as inflation and mortgage yields influence housing market behavior.
